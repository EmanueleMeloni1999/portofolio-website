Italia 2162 is an animated feature film set in a visually stunning futuristic Italy, blending adventure, drama, and hope. Produced by Chaosmonger Studio, the project launched a Kickstarter campaign but unfortunately did not reach its funding goal. Despite this, the team produced a full concept teaser — I contributed as Assets Rigger, building production-ready character and creature rigs used in the trailer.
