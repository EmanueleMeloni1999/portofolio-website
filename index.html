About Me

I am a Rigger and Technical Artist deeply passionate about my craft. Since a young age, I have been fascinated by cinema and animation, long before fully understanding the machinery behind them.

Rigging became the perfect intersection between logic and art. It allows me to merge analytical thinking, structured problem solving, and artistic sensitivity into a single discipline.

Throughout my career I have led teams, developed custom tools and proprietary systems, and optimized production pipelines to empower artists to work more efficiently and creatively.

Alongside production work, I also teach strengthening my ability to communicate complex technical ideas in a clear and production-oriented way.
